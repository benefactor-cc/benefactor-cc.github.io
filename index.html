Search intent architecture

SEO · Service pages · Content systems

Challenge

What we built

Result

The site becomes easier to crawl, easier for buyers to understand, and easier for the team to connect to qualified conversations.

Paid search alignment

SEM · Landing pages · Offer testing

Efficient Acquisition
Intent-led campaignsLanding-page fit
Challenge

Paid search gets expensive when campaigns point broad queries at generic pages and leave the sales promise disconnected from the buyer's problem.

What we built

We group campaigns by job-to-be-done, tighten ad-to-page message match, and test offers against the actions the business actually wants.

Result

Budgets move toward clearer intent, better-qualified clicks, and landing pages that make the next action obvious.
