Working Principles

Default internal stance

Commercial discipline

  • Agency fees and pass-through spend should be treated as separate budget lines.
  • Any client-funded media or software spend should have a hard ceiling and explicit ownership.
  • Anything likely to create reimbursement confusion should be documented before kickoff.

Operational preference

  • Client-owned ad accounts are preferred whenever practical.
  • Client-issued virtual cards are preferred over shared physical prepaid cards.
  • Approval thresholds and reporting cadence should be written into the commercial agreement.
